Information Collection and Use

DownloadMate does not collect personal information such as name, email, or contact details. However, to perform media downloads from platforms like YouTube, Instagram, or Twitter, we require platform-specific session cookies, which are obtained via in-app login pages.

These cookies are stored locally on your device and are used solely to authorize downloads through our FastAPI backend. They are never uploaded, stored in a cloud database, or shared with any third parties.

Backend Service Usage

When initiating a download, necessary information such as the media URL, chosen format, and relevant cookies are sent to our FastAPI backend to process the request. No data is logged or stored on our servers after the download is completed.

Log Data

In case of an error, we may collect limited diagnostic data (such as device type, OS version, or time of failure) for debugging purposes. These logs are not linked to any personal identifiers and are only used to improve app stability.

Cookies

DownloadMate uses cookies only as required to authenticate with supported platforms for downloading media. These cookies are handled within the app and are never shared outside the device. Refusing cookies may prevent successful downloads from certain platforms.

Service Providers

We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising services. All interactions happen either locally on your device or via our self-hosted backend server, which does not retain user data.
